Turning Complexity into Action

I specialize in turning complexity into action, driving strategic initiatives that align vision with execution. With a track record of leading multi-million-dollar programs, optimizing operations, and empowering cross-functional teams, I deliver innovative solutions to the most pressing organizational challenges.

As a Technology and Operations leader, I turn ideas into processes and empower people. I support C-suite executives in achieving strategic goals, delivering critical initiatives, and identifying considered solutions. I achieve results by building relationships with executive leadership and collaborating with key stakeholders to create shared understanding and consensus.
